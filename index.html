A Timeline of Cristiano Ronaldo's Achievements

  • 1985 - Cristiano Ronaldo is born on the beautiful island of Madeira, Portugal, to humble beginnings.
  • 2003 - Signed by Manchester United for a record-breaking transfer fee for a teenager, he becomes the team's star player, leading them to multiple Premier League titles and Champions League glory.
  • 2008 - Wins his first Ballon d'Or, marking the start of his reign as one of the greatest players in the world.
  • 2010 - Wins his second Ballon d'Or and leads Portugal to the Round of 16 in the World Cup.
  • 2011 - Wins his third Ballon d'Or, his first with Real Madrid, where he had transferred in 2009.
  • 2013 - Wins his fourth Ballon d'Or and leads Real Madrid to their tenth Champions League title.
  • 2016 - Wins the European Championship with Portugal, their first major international trophy.
  • 2017 - Wins his fifth Ballon d'Or, equaling the record set by his fierce rival, Lionel Messi.
  • 2019 - Wins his sixth Ballon d'Or, surpassing Messi's record
